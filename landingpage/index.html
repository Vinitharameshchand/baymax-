The Problem

Medication Non-Adherence is a Silent Crisis

Millions of patients — especially elderly individuals — struggle to consistently take their medications, leading to serious health consequences.

⚠️

Patients Forget Doses

Nearly 50% of chronic disease patients fail to take medications as prescribed, with forgetfulness being the #1 reason.

👴

Elderly Living Alone

Senior patients without daily caregiver presence are most vulnerable to dangerous missed-medication patterns.

🏥

Worsening Health

Non-adherence leads to disease progression, preventable hospitalizations, and significantly poorer health outcomes.

👨‍👩‍👧

No Family Visibility

Caregivers and family members have no real-time way to know if a loved one has taken their medications.

💸

Increased Healthcare Costs

Medication non-adherence costs the healthcare system over $300 billion annually in the U.S. alone.

🔇

Lack of Accessible Tools

Most reminder apps require complex setup and are not designed for elderly users who prefer voice-based interaction.

How It Works

Simple. Smart. Seamless.

From setup to caregiver alerts — Baymax handles the entire medication lifecycle in 6 clear steps.

01 ➕

Add Medication Reminder

The patient or caregiver adds the medication name, dosage, and daily schedule through the mobile app in seconds.

02 💾

Reminder Stored in System

The schedule is securely saved in Firebase Firestore and instantly synced across all linked patient and caregiver devices.

03 🎙️

Voice Reminder Triggers

At the scheduled time, Baymax speaks a friendly, clear reminder using Text-to-Speech — no action needed from the patient.

04 🤝

Patient Confirms Intake

The patient responds by voice command or a single button tap to confirm they have taken their medication.

05 📊

Status Recorded

The system logs the event — marked as Taken or Missed — with a precise timestamp for the adherence history.

06 📲

Family Alert Sent If Missed

If no confirmation is received, Firebase Cloud Messaging instantly sends a push notification to the designated family caregiver.

System Architecture

A Robust, Scalable Architecture

Four interconnected layers working seamlessly to keep patients safe and caregivers informed.

📱

Mobile Application Layer

The React Native app is the primary interface for patients. It processes voice commands using Speech-to-Text, plays reminders via Text-to-Speech, and surfaces medication schedules and adherence data.

↕
⚙️

Backend API Layer

The Node.js server is the core logic engine — managing reminder schedules, processing confirmation events, evaluating missed-dose conditions, and orchestrating notification triggers.

↕
🗄️

Database Layer

Firebase Firestore persists all patient profiles, medication schedules, caregiver relationships, and adherence logs. Real-time listeners enable instant cross-device synchronization.

↕
🔔

Notification Layer

Firebase Cloud Messaging delivers time-critical missed-dose alerts to caregiver devices. Reminders are triggered at precise scheduled times, ensuring consistent patient engagement.
